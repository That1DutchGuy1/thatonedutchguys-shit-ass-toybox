THE PLAYER

You are Mario! After having smoked one too many mushrooms, you've ended up somewhere that no plumber should ever be. Your only goal: reach the top floor and escape before he finds you.

THE MANSION

A cursed five-floor labyrinth that shifts and breathes on its own. It's dark. The walls close in. No map exists — no one who drew one ever made it out. Each floor is a new maze, darker and stranger than the last. Something about this place feels wrong in a way you can't name.

WEEGEE

He roams every floor. He does not stop.
Eye contact is FATAL! His origin is unknown.
His purpose is singular. You are it.
